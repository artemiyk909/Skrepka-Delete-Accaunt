DATA AND ACCOUNT DELETION

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. Below are the methods to completely remove your information from our system.

🛠️ How to Delete Your Account Manually

You can completely delete your profile at any time through the application settings.

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the "Profile" section.
  3. Click on the gear icon (Settings).
  4. Click the "Delete Account" button.

⚠️ Important: This action will instantly and permanently delete all user data. It cannot be undone.

✉️ Request Data Deletion via Email

If you cannot access your account or prefer to send a formal request, please email us.

  • Email: sehatsuksesindonesia@gmail.com
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request

Email Template:

Hello,

I am requesting the permanent deletion of my account and all associated personal data from your system.

My Account Email/ID: [Insert your email or ID here]

Thank you.

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation to your email.

PERMINTAAN PENGHAPUSAN AKUN DAN DATA

Kami menghormati privasi dan hak Anda untuk mengontrol data pribadi Anda. Berikut adalah metode untuk menghapus informasi Anda secara permanen dari sistem kami.

🛠️ Cara Menghapus Akun Secara Manual

Anda dapat menghapus profil Anda sepenuhnya kapan saja melalui pengaturan aplikasi.

  1. Masuk ke akun Anda.
  2. Buka bagian "Profil".
  3. Klik ikon roda gigi (Pengaturan).
  4. Klik tombol "Hapus Akun" (Delete Account).

⚠️ Penting: Tindakan ini akan segera dan secara permanen menghapus semua data pengguna. Tindakan ini tidak dapat dibatalkan.

✉️ Meminta Penghapusan Data via Email

Jika Anda tidak dapat mengakses akun Anda atau lebih memilih untuk mengirimkan permintaan resmi, silakan kirim email kepada kami.

  • Email: sehatsuksesindonesia@gmail.com
  • Subjek: Data Deletion Request

Template Email:

Hello,

I am requesting the permanent deletion of my account and all associated personal data from your system.

My Account Email/ID: [Masukkan email atau ID Anda di sini]

Thank you.

Kami akan memproses permintaan Anda dalam waktu 30 hari dan mengirimkan konfirmasi ke email Anda.
